Background

The Trade Lithography Company is a wholesale printing business that deals directly with other companies by selling to print brokers and other printing companies. They expanded to digital printing and provide direct mailing in-house. They do prepress, litho printing, bindery, inkjet, and postal delivery including drop shipments all within the same working day.

The client wants a complete overhaul of their company website, fixing issues from website functionality to how the overall website presents itself. In specific details, the client mainly wants us to fix up the internal website which consist of the employees and admin user screen. Scope

  • Create/modify the current employee website for a more modern and ease of use for employees
  • Filter and serach function for employees to look up existing and past information
  • Allow admins to modify employee schedules easier
  • Arrange and combine several employee site for better visual and access
  • Implement a login, logout, create, and forgot system

Proposed Solution

Using what the employer already have of his website, we plan to build and create upon that website and modify it for a more simpiler and modern look that the employer and his employees would find easier to use. Keeping in mind the needs of the employer, we plan to uphold his decisions and execute all high-proirity features necessary for the website.

Highlights

  • Modern and simple to use while keeping all the complex feature
  • Renovating and updating an old business to keep up with the ever changing technology
  • Dealing more with the employer and employee side of the website than the customer
  • Enhance experience and ease of use for a more older audience
